Frequently Asked Questions

What is Alinea Invest?

The word Alinea comes from French and it means a new line (new paragraph to be exact.)

Alinea Invest is a new way to financially empower yourself.

We are first and foremost a community sharing our investing journey together – we push each other to financially grow while simultaneously empowering one another.

Why are we building a trading platform?

Investing is overwhelming, and has been exclusive of certain demographics, especially women.

After much frustration with our own investing experiences and those around us, we wanted to create a platform to make your investing journey seamless, social, and fun.

What exactly are we building?

We are building the first ever trading platform personalized to YOU– an all in one trading platform where you can buy and sell stocks, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies that you believe in.

*We are not a robo-advisor*

What do we offer?

We will offer assets complying with a required ESG criterion. These include stocks, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies.

We offer fractional investing so you can buy a piece of any company or asset.

Why should you get early access?

Because money is power. And investing is the way to get money – follow where I’m going with this?

Seriously, we’re taking a new approach to investing and making it an interactive, social, and educational experience all for you, so get on the list!
